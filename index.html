Meet Lyda Gala

Founder & Team Leader of Gala Realty Group

Originally from Cuba and proud to call Houston home since 2014, Lyda brings a background in Hospitality and a lifelong dedication to service. Having lived across four countries, she has developed a rare ability to truly understand what clients need — not just what they ask for — and turn that insight into results.

After building expertise in customer care and sharpening strong negotiation skills, Lyda earned her real estate license in 2020 and has closed over 46 sales totaling over $20 million in value — helping families buy, sell, and rent across the greater Houston area.

With over 6 years of experience in real estate, Lyda brings an unwavering commitment to excellence, outstanding results, and concierge-level service to every client she represents. Whether guiding a buyer to their perfect home or positioning a seller for maximum value, she approaches every transaction with clarity, confidence, and a relentless dedication to exceeding expectations — from the first conversation to the closing table.

Gala Realty Group was built on one promise: the client's goals come first, always. Lyda doesn't just help clients buy and sell homes — she builds lasting relationships rooted in trust, transparency, and results that speak for themselves.

Outside of real estate, you'll find Lyda enjoying the outdoors with her two daughters and husband, or watching a good UFC fight on a Saturday night!

Bilingual in English and Spanish — because everyone deserves to feel at home.
